About Paws and Claws Pet Accessories

Paws and Claws is a well-loved pet store in Bashundhara, Dhaka, offering a wide variety of products and services to meet the needs of pets, including dogs, cats, birds, and small animals. The store specializes in high-quality pet food, grooming supplies, toys, and accessories, making it a one-stop shop for pet essentials. Known for its friendly and knowledgeable staff, Paws and Claws is dedicated to assisting pet owners with expert advice on pet care and maintenance. It's a favorite destination for pet enthusiasts in the Bashundhara area, ensuring pets are happy and well-cared for.

Feature Brands

At Paws and Claws in Bashundhara, Dhaka, we proudly offer premium pet food brands like SmartHeart, Me-O, and ALF, renowned for their high-quality, nutritious selections. SmartHeart delivers scientifically balanced nutrition for cats and dogs, Me-O provides a range of tasty and nutritious meals specially designed for cats, and ALF focuses on natural ingredients to promote optimal health for both cats and dogs. With these trusted brands, you can be sure your pets receive the best in care and nutrition.
